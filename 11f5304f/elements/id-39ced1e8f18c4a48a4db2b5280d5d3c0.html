Collective Eligibility Service ()
This service is/was used by MSM Energy for closed collective switches, where only customers that have already signed up for marketing before the start of a collective switch are eligable for the collective switch. Most recent collective switches (as of May 2021) have been open collective switches that do not have this restriction placed on customers. The service loads customer email addresses from the /components/collective-eligibility-bucket S3 bucket and then holds those email addresses in-memory, within the service's Java heap. The service exposes a service endpoint that the MSM Energy frontend can call to find out whether a given customer email address is eligable for the current collective switch. We plan to hand this over to CSI as part of the Preference Centre.
